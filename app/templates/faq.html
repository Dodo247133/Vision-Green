Frequently Asked Questions

How does the trash detection system work?

Our system uses advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to analyze CCTV footage in real-time. It can identify individuals, detect the act of throwing trash, and classify the type of waste being disposed.

How are points awarded?

Points are awarded based on the type of trash and whether it was disposed of correctly in a designated dustbin. Different types of waste have different point values, and penalties are applied for improper disposal.

What can I do with my points?

You will be able to redeem your points for a variety of rewards, such as gift cards, discounts, and exclusive merchandise. The redeemable options will be available in the "Redeem Points" section soon.

What if I believe my points were deducted incorrectly?

If you believe there has been an error in your points, you can report the issue through the "Report an Issue" page. Our team will review your case and make any necessary corrections.
